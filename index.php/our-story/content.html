Our story

Introduction

The Journey of Builder Girl and Emma: From Physiotherapy to Building Dreams.

Emma’s story isn’t your typical builder’s tale.

With 14 enriching years as an NHS Physiotherapist, her transition into the world of construction was as unexpected as it was inspired. The journey began when renovating her own home turned into a challenging endeavor. Frustrations with builders who failed to keep their promises led Emma to take matters into her own hands.

Driven by determination, she embarked on local courses and embraced hands-on learning. Her commitment to excellence and meticulous work did not go unnoticed. A neighbor, impressed with Emma’s impeccable garage conversion, sought her expertise. That single recommendation spiraled into a whirlwind of opportunities. Word of mouth about her unmatched skills and reliability began to spread like wildfire.

“Being hands on and getting involved.

Understanding multiple trades from the ground up.

Working alongside skilled tradespersons who are still working with me to this day.“

-Emma Hulme, CEO

Juggling two worlds, Emma divided her time between physiotherapy and building. However, as the demand for her exceptional services grew, she made the heart-wrenching decision to bid farewell to her physio career. She wholeheartedly dived into her newfound passion.

Thus, Builder Girl was born. Over the past 4 years, what started as a personal venture has transformed into a thriving enterprise. The team has expanded, a fleet of vehicles graces the roads, and countless homes have been touched by Builder Girl’s signature blend of quality and care.

Today, Builder Girl isn’t just a business; it’s a testament to Emma’s journey, dedication, and the belief that with passion, every brick laid can build dreams.

Our mission

Empowering homeowners with transformative building and design solutions that perfectly mirror their aspirations. At BuilderGirl, we combine expertise, creativity, and dedication to deliver spaces that resonate with individuality, functionality, and beauty

Our vision

To be the leading choice for bespoke building services, celebrated not just for our craftsmanship, but for our unwavering commitment to integrity, innovation, and client satisfaction. As advocates for women in business, we envision a future where diversity drives industry excellence.

Our values

Builder Girl is anchored in a relentless commitment to client-centric excellence, integrity, and innovation, all while championing women in business and setting unparalleled standards of quality and reliability in every project we undertake.
